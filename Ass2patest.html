60 Years of The Paralympics Games

On the Opening Ceremony of the London 1948 Olympic Games, the first competition for wheelchair athletes was named the Stoke Mandeville Games. The Stoke Mandeville Games Later became the Paralympics Games which takes place every four years with the same year and city of Olympic Games.

The increase of Participating Countries

The number of participating countries for Summer paralympic have a dramatically increase from 1960 (23) to 2020 (161). While, winter paralympic participation has sightlt increased from 1976 (16) to 2018 (49).

Total Participating Countries

The source of participation distributes in all continents. Countries such as USA, Australia, Brazil and Denmark attend most actively. Well, the participation in some countries like Russia and China are not aviable in the map.

Total Participation by Year

The plot above shows total participation by year in summer and winter paralympic. Less 1000 participation attend each winter paralympic. According to other materials, the data in 1988 might be wrong. In summer paralympic, the participation is keeping an upward trend. Additionally, in each year, there are 1000 more male than female.
