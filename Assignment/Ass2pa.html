The Paralympics Games

The visualisation is about 60 years of Paralympics Games.

The symbol of the Paralympic Games is composed of three "agitos", colored red , blue , and green , encircling a single point, on a white filed. The Paralympics Games takes place every four years with the same year and place of Olympic Games.

How the participations increase?

The game increases 10 participating countries per year.

As illustrated in the right chart, less participations attend the winter paralympic than summer paralympic. The temporary increase in 1988 might be wrong according to other materials. Additionally, in each year, there are around 1000 more men than women.

Hint: Select legend to highlight gender.

Which Countries Participate?

The source of participation distributes in almost all continents. As shown in the map, we can see the large differences of participations among countries since the participations of legend rise in a log scale.

If we zoom in, we can find that almost all Europe countries attended paralympics games. It's probaility related to the national habbies of the countries. While Aisa might be less active comparing to other continents.

As the green becomes darker, more participations in the country. Countries such as USA, Australia, Brazil and Denmark attend most actively (more than 1000 per population in total in these 15 years). While, the participations in some countries like Russia and China are not aviable in the map.

How has Australia performed?

From the charts, we can see that generally, Australia gained more silver medals than gold and bronze ones.

In 2000, Ausrtalia gain the most medals with 63 gold medals, 47 bronze medals and 39 silver medals. It took place in Spain and have the most participations (286) which can be seen from the circle size. On the contrary, the paralympic game in 1984 also got a good result with only 108 participations, with all medals over 50.

Therefore, there is a sightly relationship between participations and the number of medals. According to other materials, the reason why Ausrtalia have gained fewer medals in the beginning years is fewer disciplines opened.

Hint: Drag the x scale of years to see total medals. Click a medal category to highlight count medals by years.

Which are disciplines in 2021?

The Tokyo Paralympic games scheduled to take place in 2020, but due to the COVID-19 pandemic was rescheduled to 24 August to 5 Spetember 2021. To statisfy the most demanding tastes, the 2021 Paralympics offers 23 of best disciplines. Furthermore, it offers a total 539 events of these disciplines.

The average age of participations is around 35. The youngest person who participates the game is no more than 10 years old. Less people over 60 years old to participate 2020 paralympic game.

The aim of Paralympics is to encourage all 4000 athletes to complete. Among these participations, 1142 participations attend athletics, followed by swimming (602 participations). While other sports have fewer participations.

It is true that the disciplies of Summer Paralympics and Summer Paralympics are quite differences since they target to different people. However, both of them demonstrate the sportsmanship of the althletes.
